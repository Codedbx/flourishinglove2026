Our Love Story

It began with a friendship that quietly became forever.

Our story began when we were teenagers at Christ Embassy Charis Teens Church, Warri. From the very beginning, what stood out about her was her love for God and her passion for winning souls. Her dedication to God's work was truly inspiring.

As we served God together and spent more time around each other, our friendship naturally grew deeper. What began as admiration and friendship gradually blossomed into a beautiful relationship built on faith, purpose, and genuine love.

Today, we look back with gratitude, knowing that God was writing our story from the very beginning. What started in church as two teenagers serving God has grown into a beautiful journey of faith, friendship, and love.

On the first of August, surrounded by the people we love most, we begin our greatest chapter yet. We would be honoured to have you there.

— Flourish & Godwin
